Developmental Psychology

My name is Dr. Montano and I am a child psychologist that has earned his Docterate degree from Howard University; I have volunteered with many clinics to help children with coping with trauma and learning how to be able to express thier feelings in a safe space that I help create for them. I chose this profession because I have personally gone through trauma and did not have the resources to help cope with it in a healthy way so with this oppurtunity to share knowledge on how to cope with trauma I hope that coming to this website will help the understanding of devlopmental psychology.

I have voluntered at many places throughout my career teaching future psychlogists the ways of my techniques and skill that would be necssary when dealing with children; also I make therapy much more affordable to the low income communities since these would be the higher target for childern who go through trauma and not being able to find a safe space to let their emotions come to light about their situations. My overall goal would be to help as many kids as possible to show them that bright futures await them and though it may seem that the trauma has a hold of you when in reality it does not; so with this I conclude my staments with telling the reader that my services could be made available to them

I have worked very hard to show that mental health is very important and it all starts at a young age and with this goal I hope to change kids lives for the bettter.
